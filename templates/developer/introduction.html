Getting Started

Introduction

Cadence is an automated recurring billing and subscription lifecycle engine built specifically on top of Nomba's online payment APIs.

By sitting as an orchestration layer between your software application and Nomba's payment primitives, Cadence automatically handles tokenized card authorizations, billing periods, automatic retries (dunning logic), and webhook states on your behalf.

¡
Looking for merchant controls? Log in to the Merchant Portal to manage your active plans, generate developer tokens, and monitor subscribers.

Quickstart

Getting integration up and running takes less than 5 minutes. Follow these three core steps:

1

Generate your Developer API Key

Go to your merchant dashboard, navigate to the API Keys section, and generate a new key (e.g. cd_live_...). Include it in the HTTP headers of your server-side requests.

2

Define a Billing Plan

Define a subscription package (such as a monthly charge of ₦2,000) using the dashboard UI or by hitting the POST /api/plans endpoint.

3

Enroll a Subscriber

When a customer checks out, call the subscriptions endpoint to receive a secure Nomba checkout URL. Redirect your customer to this link to capture and authorize their card.
