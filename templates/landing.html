Automated Card Renewals

Cadence tracks subscription periods and automatically charges tokenized customer cards using Nomba's Payment APIs at the end of each billing cycle.

Failed Charge Escalations

If a renewal fails, our built-in scheduler escalates payment retries over a custom period (e.g. 1d, 3d, 7d) and manages subscription grace periods automatically.

Robust REST APIs & Portals

Includes full merchant dashboards, developer docs, and self-service passwordless billing portals where subscribers can manage their cards or cancel.
